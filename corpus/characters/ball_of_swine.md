## Canonical Description

The Ball of Swine is a formidable entity that can roll and move, capable of getting stuck in certain situations. It has been referred to as both a level 15 Borough Boss and an alias known as The Porkchop Express. Its actions have resulted in harm to others, such as smashing Yolanda in the head.
