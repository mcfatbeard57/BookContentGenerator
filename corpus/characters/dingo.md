## Canonical Description

Dingo is a formidable and efficient predator who has earned nicknames such as 'one-eyed dingo' due to her unique appearance. She is known for her ability to consume and digest other creatures, including larger prey like Ralph and even a boss. Her actions are characterized by a matter-of-fact approach to violence.
