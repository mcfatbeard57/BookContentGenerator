## Canonical Description

Frenzied Gerbils are part earth rodent, part the embodiment of death. They can be encountered on the fifth or seventh floors. Some individuals, like Ralph, have dedicated their existence to fighting and training.

## Physical Traits

- part earth rodent
- part the embodiment of death

## Personality

- dedicated to fighting and training
