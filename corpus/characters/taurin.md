## Canonical Description

Taurin is a protagonist who has been navigating the dungeon with the aid of an AI. Some individuals, including his own piglets, question his abilities and suggest he has had it easy due to this assistance. This criticism implies that Taurin's success may be attributed more to luck than skill.
