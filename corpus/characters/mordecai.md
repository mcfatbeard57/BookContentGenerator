## Canonical Description

Mordecai is a character who serves as a trainer and mentor to the protagonist. He emphasizes the importance of using bare-knuckle skills, which are comparable in power to unenchanted weapons. Mordecai also provides guidance on scroll usage, suggesting that party members with higher intelligence should read scrolls.
