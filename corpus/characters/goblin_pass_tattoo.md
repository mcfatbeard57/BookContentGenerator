## Canonical Description

The Goblin Pass tattoo is a permanent mark that removes automatic goblin hostility and allows for free passage through goblin-controlled territory in the dungeon. It cannot be hidden without purchasing a coverup sleeve, and will show through any armor worn.

## Abilities

- removes automatic goblin hostility
- grants free passage through goblin-controlled territory
