## Canonical Description

The boss room, also known as the main boss chamber, is a confined space with a unique feature: a tube runs along its ceiling from an adjacent room, curving down into a dirty aluminum bathtub. The room's design and purpose are focused on containing and challenging adventurers. Its appearance is functional rather than ornate.
