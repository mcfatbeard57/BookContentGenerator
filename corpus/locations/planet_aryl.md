## Canonical Description

Planet Aryl is a location with a massive arena building, similar to the Roman Coliseum, that serves as a venue for the Battle for the Planet Aryl event. The arena features multiple floors, mazes, and a water-filled level. The exterior of the building is marked with the sign 'The Squim Conglomerate Presents'.
