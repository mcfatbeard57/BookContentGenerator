## Canonical Description

The Squim Conglomerate Presents is a large-scale entertainment organization that hosts events in an enormous arena, featuring multiple floors, mazes, and water-filled levels. Their signature event is the Battle for the Planet Aryl. The exterior of their building is emblazoned with their name and event title.
