## Canonical Description

The Training Guilds are a group of organizations that provide training and guidance to individuals, with a registered guide assigned to each member. They have multiple locations, with access restricted based on floor level. Members can return to their registered guide after completing training.

guild

- Provide training and guidance
- Assign registered guides
