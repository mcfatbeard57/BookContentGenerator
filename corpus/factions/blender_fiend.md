## Canonical Description

The Blender Fiend is a mysterious organization that appears to be comprised of individuals with the ability to increase their collective strength after 'blending' with other entities. They have demonstrated an alarming rate of growth, having defeated 15,000 crawlers in a single day. Their actions pose a significant threat to the ecosystem.
