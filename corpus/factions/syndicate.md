## Canonical Description

The Syndicate is a governing body that oversees the World Dungeon and its inhabitants. They provide tutorial guilds to guide new players and have strict rules regarding production companies, including those tied to sponsors.

## Faction Type

government

## Goals

- regulate the World Dungeon
- monitor production companies

## Traits

- monopolistic control over the World Dungeon
- close ties with production sponsors
