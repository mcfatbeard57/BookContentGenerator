## Canonical Description

The Silver Boxes are a patron organization that provides boxes containing various items to adventurers in exchange for payment. They offer silver and gold boxes, with higher-tier boxes typically containing better items.

## Faction Type

guild

## Goals

- Provide valuable items to patrons
- Generate revenue through box sales

## Traits

- Accepts payment in the form of silver or gold boxes
- Boxes are categorized by material (bronze, silver, gold)
