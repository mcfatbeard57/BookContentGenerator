## Canonical Description

The Dungeon Crawler is a group of adventurers who explore and navigate dungeons, often sponsored by patrons to retrieve valuable items or information. Their numbers are known to fluctuate significantly as they progress through their missions, with many failing to return from deeper levels. As a result, few sponsors support crawlers earlier than the fourth floor.
