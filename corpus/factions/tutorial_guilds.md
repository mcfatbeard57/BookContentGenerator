## Canonical Description

The Tutorial Guilds are organizations that provide guidance and assistance to adventurers entering the World Dungeon. They can be found on levels 1 through 3 of the dungeon, offering valuable support to those seeking to navigate its challenges.

## Faction Type

guild

## Goals

- Provide guidance and assistance to adventurers
- Support navigation of the World Dungeon

## Traits

- Multiple locations throughout the World Dungeon
- Available on levels 1-3
