## Canonical Description

The Engineers are a group of skilled craftsmen and inventors who specialize in creating innovative solutions to complex problems. They are known for their resourcefulness and ability to repurpose materials, as seen in their use of coal and fur in their work. Their focus on practical applications has earned them a reputation as reliable problem-solvers.
