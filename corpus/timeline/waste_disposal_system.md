## Canonical Description

The party discovers that every floor has a waste disposal system, which attracts creatures to feed on corpses. If no bodies are nearby, these creatures will hunt crawlers instead.

## Event Type

discovery

## Participants

- Carl
- party

## Consequences

- The party is aware of the waste disposal system's effect on attracting creatures.
- They must be cautious not to become prey when no corpses are nearby.
